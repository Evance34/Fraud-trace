Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

Answers to some of the questions we receive most often.

Will my information remain confidential?

Yes. Information submitted through our complaint form is handled confidentially and used only for reviewing your report and communicating with you regarding your case.

How long before someone contacts me?

We aim to review new submissions promptly. Response times may vary depending on the complexity of the report and the volume of cases.

Can I upload evidence?

Yes. You may upload screenshots, receipts, emails, PDFs, chat records, and other supporting documents when submitting your report.

Do I need to pay to submit a complaint?

No. Submitting a complaint through our website is free.
