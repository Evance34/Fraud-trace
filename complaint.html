File a Fraud Complaint

Complete the secure form below with as much detail as possible. Supporting documents can be uploaded in the next step.

Victim Information

Fields marked with * are required.

Accepted file types: JPG, PNG, PDF, DOC, DOCX, TXT and ZIP. Maximum upload size will be enforced by the server.

Your Privacy Matters

Information you submit is handled confidentially and is reviewed only for the purpose of assessing your complaint and communicating with you about your case.

What Happens Next?

  1. Your complaint is securely received.
  2. Our team performs an initial review.
  3. If additional information is needed, we will contact you using the email address or phone number you provided.
  4. Where appropriate, we can discuss available reporting options based on the circumstances you described.

Helpful Tips

  • Upload clear screenshots.
  • Include receipts or payment confirmations.
  • Provide email headers if available.
  • Do not alter documents before uploading.
  • Include dates whenever possible.

Important Notice

If you believe you are in immediate danger or a crime is currently in progress, contact your local emergency services or law enforcement immediately.

Need Help Before Submitting?

If you're unsure which fraud category applies or have questions about the form, contact us before submitting your complaint.
