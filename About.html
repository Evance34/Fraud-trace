About Fraud Trace

Helping individuals organize fraud reports, preserve evidence, and receive guidance through a secure and professional reporting process.

WHO WE ARE

Dedicated to Helping Victims of Fraud

Fraud Trace is an independent fraud reporting and case support service dedicated to helping individuals document suspected scams, organize supporting evidence, and submit detailed reports for review.

Our goal is to make the reporting process easier by providing a structured, secure, and user-friendly platform where victims can explain what happened, upload relevant documentation, and receive follow-up communication from our team.

Whether the incident involves online fraud, investment schemes, romance scams, identity theft, or other forms of financial deception, our focus is on helping clients present their information clearly and efficiently.

COMMON CASES

Fraud Types We Help Document

Investment Fraud

Cryptocurrency Scams

Email impersonation or invoice fraud targeting businesses and organizations.

QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to some of the questions we receive most often.

Is my information kept confidential?

Yes. Information submitted through Fraud Trace is handled confidentially and is used for reviewing your report and communicating with you regarding your case.

What happens after I submit my complaint?

Your report is reviewed by our team. If additional information is needed, we will contact you using the email address or phone number you provided.

Can I upload screenshots and documents?

Yes. You can upload screenshots, receipts, emails, PDFs, chat logs and other supporting evidence with your complaint.

Is there a fee to submit a complaint?

No. Submitting a complaint through our website is currently free.
